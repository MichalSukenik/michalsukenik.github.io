Visual Scripting

I have been using Unreal Engines visual scripting (blueprints) for about one year now and I have loved every minute of it. It brings me great satisfaction to complete a project to the best of my ability and come up with my own way of doing certain mechanics in games. I especially love going above and beyond in projects where I can show of these skills.

Optimised Search Mechanic

This mechanic and level was done as a part of a University project inside of a very basic framework given to us. I decided to impliment a search functionality. The player can walk up to different objects and search them for resources.
By adding some aditional blueprints I managed to allow the creator to change the time taken to search and the resource output without having to go back into the blueprints. I had people play test my whole level including my professors and they all gave positive feedback.

Minecart

This gameplay system was done in the same University Project framework. I created a minecart that is used to open up the next part of the level.
The minecart requires the player to have powered the rails as well as place the explosive inside before it takes off. I then used the explosion to hide from the player when the door gets removed.
