About Me

My name is Michal Sukenik and I am a hard working dedicated student at Staffordshire University. My interest in games was sparked as a child when I could play games such as Kerbal Space Program and Forza Horizon for hours on end. This then further expanded my love and interest for a larger variety of games. It would mean a lot to me if I could spark this interest into other people. I now play many game genres like FPS, Simulators, RPGs and Horrors. As I grew older this interest in games turned into an interest in how these games I love are made.
