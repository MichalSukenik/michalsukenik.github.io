Games Jams

I love participating in game jams as it further improves my skills like teamwork and communication. It also gives me more experience with creating games.

Freshers Jam 2022

This was my first ever game jam before any classes from University. "After School Detention" was our idea as we had no restrictions. I used Unity for this jam which was a fairly new engine to me. It was a challenge being the only programmer, however the game still had a win, fail and coin system implimented.

Kenney Jam 2023

The theme for this jam was "Exploration." We had a very small time window for unrelated reasons. However, even though we were very restricted I believe we did a good job. I also attempted to create a save and load system however I didn't manage to get this done in time.

The GameDev.tv Game Jam 2023

For this jam the theme was "Life In Two Dimensions." Me and my partner came up with a reality/dream game. The majority of our time was spent on 3D modelling as we were missing a team member, however I definitely enjoyed creating the movement script from scratch as well as the scene transitions. If we were to redo this I would put much more time into some unique mechanics of the game.
